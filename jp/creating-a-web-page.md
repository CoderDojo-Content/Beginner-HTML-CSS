1. Why do you need two tags? You have an **opening** and a **closing** tag to tell the browser where things start and end. So for a paragraph, the opening `<p>` tag says "here comes some text that I want you to display as a paragraph". The closing `</p>` tag tells the browser where the paragraph ends. Everything in between the `<body>` and `</body>` tags is your web page.

   * Notice how the** closing **tag always has a forward slash, `/`.
   * The tags define different pieces of a page, for example paragraphs and headings, or the body; these pieces are all called **elements**. Think of them as building blocks.
